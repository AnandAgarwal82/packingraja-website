Our Policies

Order Payment and Ownership

50% advance payment is mandatory for all custom-made orders. The balance payment must be cleared before dispatch. Goods remain the property of PackingRaja until full payment is received.

Returns and Replacements

Goods once delivered will only be replaced if found defective due to manufacturing defects, and the defect is reported within 5 days of delivery.

Shipping Delays

PackingRaja is not liable for any delays or failures in delivery caused by circumstances beyond our control, including courier delays, weather, or force majeure events.

Payment Verification

Please always verify bank account details before transferring funds. PackingRaja will not be responsible for payments made to incorrect or fraudulent accounts.

Sampling Costs

Sampling costs may or may not be adjusted against the final invoice depending on the case.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising out of invoices or transactions will be subject to the jurisdiction of Jaipur, Rajasthan courts exclusively.

Taxes

All taxes, including GST or other applicable taxes, are included or excluded as specifically mentioned on invoices.

GST Compliance

GSTINs submitted must be valid and active. Mismatched or invalid GSTINs may result in invoice cancellation or GST input credit denial.
